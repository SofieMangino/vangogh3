The sadness will last forever.

Head of a Skeleton with a Cigarette was one of Van Goghs early paintings. He had been studying anatomy at a school of art where skeletons were not tpically painted but drawn. Van Gogh created this painting as a juvenile joke.

Although Cafe Terrace at Night is a painting that takes place during the evening, the painting is a devoid of the color black. Van Gogh had written in a letter to his sister stating that the night sky is richer in color than the day.

Van Gogh had done 35 self portraits during his short years of an artist. During his time in Paris from 1886-88 he was short of money. He wanted to practice painting people but had a hard time hiring models. So his easiest solution was to paint himself.
